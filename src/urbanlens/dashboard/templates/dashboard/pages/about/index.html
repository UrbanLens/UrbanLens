{# -- What is UrbanLens ------------------------------------------------------ #}
What is UrbanLens?

Your personal atlas of forgotten places.

Urban explorers accumulate knowledge - locations, access routes, structural notes, hazards, history - across years of fieldwork. That knowledge typically lives in scattered notes, private DMs, and memory. UrbanLens gives it a permanent, organised home.

Every pin you drop is yours, and stays that way until you act to share it. You decide what to share, who can see it, and how much detail is visible. The platform is built around the understanding that some places deserve to be documented without being advertised.

Who built it?

One developer, not a company.

UrbanLens is built and maintained by one person. It started as a personal tool - a way to search, document, save, and share locations and research with a small circle of friends - and grew into the platform it is now.

The goal was never to launch a product. It was to make a place where one explorer and their friends could search, document, save, and share what they'd found - privately, deliberately, and on their own terms. That's still what UrbanLens is: a tool made by an explorer, for the people they explore with. It just happens to have room for you too.

{# -- Core values ---------------------------------------------------------- #}
How it's built

Responsible by design.

01

Private by default

Every pin is private. Sharing is opt-in and granular - by friend, by trip, or not at all - so your pins don't go public by accident.

02

Hazard notes on every pin

Pins carry structural hazard notes and vulnerability ratings. We encourage users to record and share safety information, so they never explore alone.

03

A living archive

Every location gets a wiki page for history, architecture, and access notes - documentation that outlasts the building itself. Participation in the wiki is opt-in, always.

04

Built for your circle

Trip planning, RSVPs, and shared itineraries make it easy to loop in people you trust, without broadcasting a location to anyone else.

05

Safety tools

Features like safety check-ins, and immediate access to community research, make it easier to stay safe, and to get help when you need it.

06

No ads, no data sales

We don't sell location data or run ads. Pins, photos, and notes exist to run the platform - nothing else.

Curious what's behind these choices? Read our values.

map

Interactive map

Drop pins on any location worldwide. Add photos, notes, visit dates, priority ratings, and vulnerability assessments. Filter by tags and categories to find what you need instantly.

  • Custom tags, categories, and status badges
  • Satellite, street, and topographic base layers
  • Priority & vulnerability star ratings
  • GPS coordinates extracted from photo EXIF data
