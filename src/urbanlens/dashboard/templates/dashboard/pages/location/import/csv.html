  1. Go to Google Takeout and sign in with the Google account that has your saved places.
  2. Click Deselect all, then scroll down and check Saved (this covers your Google Maps lists). To also import driving-directions history, check My Activity too - open its options and set the Maps output format to HTML.
  3. Click Next step, choose how you'd like to receive the export (an emailed download link is simplest), and click Create export.
  4. Google will email you a download link, usually within a few minutes. Download the .zip file it sends you.
  5. Come back here and drop that .zip file into the upload area below - no need to unzip it first.
