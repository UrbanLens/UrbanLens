Friends are added as chips by clicking their avatar or by typing their name into the text input (autocompleted); email addresses are added as chips by typing them and pressing Enter. Reused for both check-in defaults and individual check-ins. Context: `contacts` (existing (contact_profile, email, name) tuples to prefill), `connections` (the profile's friends), and optionally `contact_status` (dict keyed by contact_profile.id or email -> {"label", "class"}, shown as a badge next to each chip - only passed on the check-in detail page, which is also the only caller that ever passes `locked=True`/`collapsible=True`), `locked` (drops the remove button/avatar-picker/input entirely once contacts have already been notified - see SafetyCheckin.contacts_locked), and `collapsible` (starts collapsed to a plain read-only chip list behind an "Edit" button rather than showing the add/remove controls inline right away - only meaningful when not `locked`). {% endcomment %}
