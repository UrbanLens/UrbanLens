help What are aliases and "Nicknames"?

Aliases are alternate names - former names, local nicknames, or codenames. They are used when you search for your own pins, and also when we contact external sources, like google searches.

Check Don't use in web searches to make an alias a nickname. Nicknames are unofficial names that won't improve search results. Like "The Lounge", "The Office with a Bad Smell", "Rooftop Hangout", or "John's Place". They will still be used when you search for your own pins on the site, but they won't ever leave the site for external searches.
