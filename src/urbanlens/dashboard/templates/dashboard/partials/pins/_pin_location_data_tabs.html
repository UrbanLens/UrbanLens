{% comment %} Location Data card: OpenStreetMap (Nominatim), Photon, and Building Characteristics used to render as three separate, near-identically-styled standalone cards with no explanation of how they relate - two of them ("OpenStreetMap" and "Photon") both reverse-geocode the same underlying OSM data via independent services and can surface different amounts of detail. Grouping them as tabs of one card makes that relationship visible instead of implying three unrelated data sources. Unlike _pin_plugin_tabs.html this isn't a generic reusable tab strip: Nominatim has its own dedicated route/template (PinController.nominatim_info has side effects - it can auto-add an alias/link from OSM data) rather than being a plain InfoPanelSource, so it can't flow through the generic pin.panel dispatch every other tab here uses. That one tab is hardcoded; the rest come from PinController.view's location_data_tabs (see _LOCATION_DATA_PLUGIN_TABS). Context: pin - the Pin being viewed location_data_tabs - list of {key, label, icon} dicts for the InfoPanelSource tabs (Photon, Building Characteristics), in tab order {% endcomment %}
