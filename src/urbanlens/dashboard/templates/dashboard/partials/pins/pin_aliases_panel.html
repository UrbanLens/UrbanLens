help What are aliases and "Nickname only"?

Aliases are the other names a place goes by - former names, local nicknames, or codenames. They help you find this pin in search, and they improve the results pulled from external sources.

Nickname only keeps an alias private to UrbanLens: it still works in your own searches, but it is never included in queries sent to external services (Google, Wikipedia, etc). Use it for names the wider internet shouldn't see. The person button on each alias toggles this.
